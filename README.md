# FATEDFORTRESS (EXPNET)

**A community of builders who ship.**

*"Build with people who actually ship."*

---

## What This Is

FatedFortress (public brand: EXPNET) is an attempt to build something different. A platform where:

- **Your work speaks.** Self-reported skills mean nothing. Real contribution tells the truth.
- **Privacy is real.** You control when you're visible. No performance tax for being yourself.
- **Trust is earned, not hoarded.** What you do recently matters. We don't believe in permanent status.
- **Teams form around execution.** Not resumes. Not credentials. Verified contribution.

This is not a finished product. It's an experiment in what collaboration could be.

---

## The Problem We See

Modern collaboration platforms fail developers:

| Problem | What Happens Instead |
|---------|---------------------|
| **Visibility over contribution** | Platforms reward self-promotion. We reward actual work. |
| **Activity conflated with value** | High activity ‚â† high value. We track what you ship. |
| **Forced self-promotion** | Credentials before participation. We let work precede visibility. |
| **Identity coercion** | Contribute without exposure. Privacy is a feature. |
| **Trust manipulation** | Escape reputation through identity changes. We track accountability. |
| **Coordination failure** | Talent exists but doesn't connect. We try to fix this. |

## What We Actually Deliver

We're honest about what this is:

| Feature | What It Is | What It Isn't |
|---------|------------|---------------|
| **XP-Based Reputation** | Experience points from verified contributions | A replacement for all reputation |
| **Visibility Modes** | ANON (pseudonymous) or OFF (visible) | Perfect anonymity |
| **AI Matching** | Algorithm proposes collaborators | Perfect team composition |
| **Trust Decay** | Recent contribution weights more | Punishment for life events |
| **Task System** | Clear scope, verified completion | All possible work captured |

**We are building this. It will evolve. We will make mistakes.**

## Core Philosophy

### What We Believe

1. **"Nothing is permanent without continued signal"**
   - Trust decays without ongoing contribution
   - But: sabbatical and life events are real
   - We are exploring ways to handle this humanly

2. **"Anonymity protects you from peers‚Äînot from adjudication"**
   - Privacy from other humans
   - Accountability to the system (for disputes, enforcement)
   - We cannot promise protection from all possible futures

### What We're Unsure About

The [`PHILOSOPHY_RECONSIDERED.md`](docs/PHILOSOPHY_RECONSIDERED.md) document contains our critical self-audit:

- **Telemetry limits.** We observe behavior, but not everything. Observable ‚â† complete.
- **Verification is circular.** Who verifies the verifiers? We don't have perfect answers.
- **Matching is probabilistic.** Teams may fail for reasons no system captures.
- **Depth vs. breadth.** The system may favor frequent small contributions over deep work.
- **Recovery is hard.** Once trust decays, rebuilding requires opportunity.

**We're building this. We'll learn. We'll adapt.**

## The Adventuring Party Model (A Heuristic)

Teams need composition, not just skills. We think in terms of:

- **Architect** ‚Äî Sees the system whole
- **Builder** ‚Äî Translates design into code
- **Guardian** ‚Äî Tests, reviews, protects quality
- **Navigator** ‚Äî Coordinates, communicates, aligns
- **Mentor** ‚Äî Elevates others, transfers knowledge
- **Patron** ‚Äî Provides resources (opt-in, voluntary)

**This is a heuristic, not a rigid schema.** Real teams are messier. We use this as a guide, not a law.
